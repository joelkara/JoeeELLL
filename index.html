About Fatphobia

Fatphobia is a form of discrimination and prejudice against people who are overweight or obese. It is perpetuated by societal norms and expectations that promote thinness and stigmatize larger bodies.

Effects of Fatphobia

  • Poor body image and self-esteem
  • Eating disorders and disordered eating
  • Health disparities and discrimination in healthcare
  • Bullying and social exclusion

Body Positivity

Body positivity is a movement that promotes self-love and acceptance of all body types, including larger bodies. It challenges societal norms and expectations that promote thinness and encourages individuals to embrace their unique shapes and sizes.
